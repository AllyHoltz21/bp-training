Take the Patient's Blood Pressure

Procedure: Put on headphones. Press Pump repeatedly to inflate the cuff above the suspected systolic pressure (typically 180–200 mmHg). Then press Open Valve to release pressure slowly (~2–3 mmHg/sec) while listening through the stethoscope. Note the pressure at the first audible Korotkoff sound (systolic) and at which they disappear (diastolic).
